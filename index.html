Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DoubleTap Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Tap Rush mobile game ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Tap Rush does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.).

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Best score and current score
  • In-game coin balance
  • Sound preference (on/off)
  • Daily reward streak and last claim date
  • Selected language (English / Spanish)

This data never leaves your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Tap Rush displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information and use cookies or similar technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your device settings and applicable law.

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices.

You can opt out of personalized advertising on Android at any time via: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Children's Privacy

Tap Rush is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for ad delivery

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
